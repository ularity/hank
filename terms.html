Program Description

Hank is a personal AI assistant that sends SMS/text messages to the account owner. Messages may include notifications, reminders, calendar alerts, weather updates, task updates, and general informational communications. Messages are sent from +1 (307) 441-3111.

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to approximately 10 messages per day depending on your notification preferences and assistant activity.

By providing your phone number and configuring the Hank AI Assistant, you consent to receive recurring text messages from this service.

Opt-Out

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP to any message at any time. You will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent unless you opt back in.
